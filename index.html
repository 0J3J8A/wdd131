Bogotá, Colombia

Bogota is the capital of Colombia and its most populous city. In 2025, it has a population of 7,937,898 inhabitants. It is located 2,582 meters above sea level. Its total surface area is 1,636.35 km². It has an oceanic climate (Cfb), with temperate temperatures. The city was founded on August 6, 1538. It is divided into 20 localities and more than 1,900 neighborhoods.
